### Conversation 1: Weather Chat

**Colleague:** Beautiful weather today, isn't it?

**Colleague:** I know! It was so gloomy last week.

**You:** The rain was nice for a couple of days, but then it got too much.

**Colleague:** Exactly. My clothes weren't drying and everything felt damp.

**You:** Same problem here. I'm glad it's sunny now.

**Colleague:** Me too. Perfect weather for the weekend.
